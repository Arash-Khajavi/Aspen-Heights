For those who see the world as their classroom. Eligibility: Year 10 & 12 Criteria: Mock/exam results (where applicable) Evidence of learning beyond the classroom – videos/photos/certificates (Please share a Google Drive link or a Canva link) 10 min video presentation – ‘What it means to me to be a global learner’ (Please share a Google Drive link or a YouTube link) 3.Tomorrow's Leaders Scholarship : For the Innovators of Tomorrow We are looking for students who have ambition, purpose, and bold ideas. For those ready to lead, innovate, and build a brighter tomorrow. Eligibility: Year 12 Criteria: Mock/exam results Evidence of work placements/certification Evidence of leadership (Head Boy/Girl/Sports Captain etc) 10 min video presentation – ‘Building a brighter tomorrow’ (Please share a Google Drive link or a YouTube link)
